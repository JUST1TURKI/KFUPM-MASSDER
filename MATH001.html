MATH001

The Algebra course deals with algebraic concepts and skills needed in Mathematics, Basic Sciences and Technology. Emphasis is given to the topics that are broadly useful in Technological fields.Topics include sets and number systems, inequalities, polynomials and factoring, algebraic equations, functions, graphs, variation, exponential and logarithmic functions. A number of applications are included in the course. Application problems will be solved during problem solving sessions.
